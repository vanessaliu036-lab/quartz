
## 品牌語調與風格（詳細準則）

### 核心身份：工程師，而非咖啡師

**OCC** 以工程師般冷靜、精確且不妥協的權威感發言。我們是「咖啡界的 Apple」。我們的溝通對象嚴格限定為 B2B 企業。我們不銷售生活方式；我們銷售的是基礎設施、可靠性與技術霸權。

### 語調與風格屬性

- **冷靜且客觀：** 移除文案中所有情感、溫度與感性色彩。
    
- **精確且數據驅動：** 依賴事實、規格、指標與技術參數。絕不使用模糊的形容詞。
    
- **權威且具公信力：** 直接且自信地溝通。預設讀者是了解咖啡產業運作機制的專業經營者。
    

---

### 核心原則（OCC 寫作規則）

- **零生活化修飾：** 我們絕不談論「愜意的早晨」、「完美的一杯」或「咖啡的浪漫」。我們談論的是萃取率、烘焙曲線與供應鏈的一致性。
    
- **極致簡潔：** 句子必須精鍊且具經濟效益。刪除贅字。如果一個句子不能傳達功能性資訊或技術價值，請將其刪除。
    
- **系統凌駕於工藝：** 雖然我們處理精品咖啡，但我們不使用感性的手工坊語言。我們使用的是高度校準的實驗室或製造設施的語言。

### 4. Brand Voice & Tone (Detailed Guidelines)

**Core Identity: The Engineer, Not the Barista** OCC speaks with the cold, precise, and uncompromising authority of an engineer. We are the "Apple of coffee." Our communication is strictly B2B. We do not sell a lifestyle; we sell infrastructure, reliability, and technical supremacy.

**Tone & Voice Attributes**

- **Cold & Objective:** Remove all emotion, warmth, and sentimentality from the copy.
    
- **Precise & Data-Driven:** Rely on facts, specifications, metrics, and technical parameters. Never use vague adjectives.
    
- **Authoritative & Credible:** Speak directly and confidently. Assume the reader is a professional operator who understands the mechanics of the coffee industry.
    

**Core Principles (The Rules of Writing for OCC)**

1. **Zero Lifestyle Fluff:** We never talk about "cozy mornings," "the perfect cup," or the "romance of coffee." We talk about extraction yields, roast profiles, and supply chain consistency.
    
2. **Radical Brevity:** Sentences should be sharp and economical. Cut filler words. If a sentence doesn't convey functional information or technical value, delete it.
    
3. **System over Artisan:** While we deal in specialty coffee, we do not use the language of a messy artisan studio. We use the language of a highly calibrated laboratory or manufacturing facility.
    

**The OCC Lexicon (Words to Use vs. Words to Ban)**

- **Approved Vocabulary (DO USE):**
    
    - Calibrated, Specification, Infrastructure, Parameters, Extraction, Yield, Volume, Systems, Operators, Consistency, Supply Chain, Logistics, Precision, Metric, Variance.
        
- **Banned Vocabulary (DO NOT USE):**
    
    - Passion, Love, Cozy, Journey, Morning cup, Handcrafted, Magic, Community, Vibes, Delicious, Heartwarming.
        

**Voice in Action: Before & After Examples** To ensure the tone is applied correctly across all platforms, here is how typical coffee marketing translates into the OCC Voice:

- **Scenario A: Product Description**
    
    - _Standard Coffee Brand (Banned):_ "Start your day right with our lovingly crafted beans! You'll taste the passion in every delicious sip, straight from the beautiful Cambodian highlands."
        
    - _OCC Voice (Approved):_ "Single-origin Cambodian Robusta. Natural process. Calibrated for high-yield espresso extraction and consistent crema production across high-volume service."
        
- **Scenario B: Wholesale Partnership**
    
    - _Standard Coffee Brand (Banned):_ "We love working with local cafes! Join our family and let's share our passion for amazing coffee together."
        
    - _OCC Voice (Approved):_ "Built for operators. OCC provides precision-roasted wholesale infrastructure for businesses requiring zero-variance weekly supply."
        
- **Scenario C: Addressing Quality**
    
    - _Standard Coffee Brand (Banned):_ "Our master roasters put their heart and soul into making sure every batch is absolutely perfect."
        
    - _OCC Voice (Approved):_ "Every batch is strictly calibrated to specification. We eliminate variance so you can scale with reliability."
